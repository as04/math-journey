How to contribute a lesson

Every lesson is a single self-contained HTML file in lessons/ that links ../styles.css. No build step, no frameworks. The pattern is:

  1. One concept per lesson.
  2. A picture (canvas or SVG) at the top.
  3. Controls (sliders, inputs, buttons) that visibly change the picture.
  4. A short explanation underneath — enough to read in five minutes.
  5. Self-test or self-check at the bottom.

Copy any existing lesson as a template. Open it in your browser to iterate. Push to git. Done.
